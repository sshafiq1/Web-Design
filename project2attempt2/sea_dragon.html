In Which A Pirate

Runs Into A Sea Dragon

Boldbeard ripped at his beard in astonishment. What kind of creature was this?

The sea dragon roared and blew flames at the ship, which would have been burnt to ashes if it weren't for the dragon's poor aim.

Frustrated, the sea dragon lashed out with his tail, causing a huge wave to wash over the ship.

Do Boldbeard and his crew survive?
